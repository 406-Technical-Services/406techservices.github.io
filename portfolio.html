Wi-Fi · Cloud Monitoring · Starlink

Rural Resort Mesh Network Rebuild

9-building property, zero cellular coverage, safety-critical connectivity — Swan Lake, MT

Problem

A 9-building resort in a cell-dead zone had Starlink signal so degraded that the worst cabins were measuring as low as 6 Mbps — and guests couldn't reliably reach emergency services. The original mesh topology spread across multiple wireless hops with the dish misaligned, and the owner had zero visibility into whether the network was up or down.

Work

Ran a professional wireless survey with NetSpot across all 9 buildings. Relocated the primary Starlink dish to a central hub (Pump House) with clear line of sight to every building, eliminating relay hops. All 9 buildings now backhaul wirelessly to a single hub — no multi-hop signal degradation. Then built a custom cloud monitoring service in Python that polls dish health every 60 seconds, tracks per-building reachability, and auto-sends alerts and a branded monthly PDF report.

Result

All 9 buildings now deliver 25–268 Mbps. Guests can reach emergency services from anywhere on the property. The owner receives automated alerts and a monthly uptime report without doing anything. Completed in two site visits.

Network Security · Cabling · PCI Compliance

Restaurant & Casino Network Audit

No documentation, unidentified cabling, cameras offline, and a PCI compliance gap — Seeley Lake, MT

Problem

A busy restaurant, bar, and casino had no IT documentation, completely unidentified cabling, multiple security cameras offline or misaligned, POS hardware running legacy software with no active support path, and payment systems exposed by a PCI compliance gap — old card readers were storing full credit card numbers.

Work

Full cabling audit with as-built documentation. Identified the PCI compliance gap and notified the client. Repaired offline cameras — realigned a dislodged P2P wireless bridge and identified a water-damaged outdoor bridge unit for replacement. Deployed a UPS during a live power outage mid-engagement to keep POS running. Designed a 6-VLAN SonicWall architecture for full network segmentation: Surveillance, POS, ATM, Office, Guest, and Gaming.

Result

Cameras back online, cabling documented, PCI exposure identified and reported to the client. POS kept alive during a live power outage. A 6-VLAN network redesign is staged for the next maintenance window.
