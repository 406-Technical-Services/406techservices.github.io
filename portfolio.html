Wi-Fi · Cloud Monitoring · Starlink

Rural Resort Mesh Network Rebuild

9-building property, zero cellular coverage, safety-critical connectivity — Swan Lake, MT

Problem

A 9-cabin resort in a cell-dead zone had Starlink signal so degraded that guests in the farthest cabins were getting 6 Mbps and couldn't reliably reach emergency services. The original mesh topology had too many wireless hops, and the owner had zero visibility into whether the network was up or down.

Work

Ran a professional wireless survey with NetSpot to map signal propagation across all 9 buildings. Redesigned the topology to a single-hub architecture and relocated the primary Starlink dish to eliminate multi-hop signal loss. Backhauled all 9 buildings wirelessly across three coverage zones. Then built a custom cloud monitoring service — Python, Starlink gRPC API, Oracle Cloud — that polls dish health every 60 seconds, tracks per-cabin reachability, and auto-sends alerts and a branded monthly PDF report.

Result

All 9 cabins now deliver 25–268 Mbps. Guests can reach emergency services from any building on the property. The owner gets automated uptime alerts and a monthly report without doing anything. Completed in two site visits.

Network Security · Cabling · PCI Compliance

Restaurant & Casino Network Audit

No documentation, mystery cabling, 13 cameras offline, and a PCI compliance gap — Seeley Lake, MT

Problem

A busy restaurant, bar, and casino had no IT documentation, completely unidentified cabling, all 13 security cameras offline or misaligned, POS systems running on an expired support contract, and payment and guest traffic on the same unsegmented network — a PCI compliance exposure.

Work

Full cabling audit with as-built documentation. Identified and escalated a PCI compliance gap through the payment processor. Repaired all 13 cameras — bad crimps, realigned a P2P wireless bridge, replaced a water-damaged outdoor bridge unit. Deployed a UPS mid-engagement during a live power outage to keep POS running. Designed a 6-VLAN SonicWall architecture for full PCI-compliant segmentation: Surveillance, POS, Office, Guest, Entertainment, and Management.

Result

All cameras live and documented. PCI exposure identified and remediation engaged through the payment processor. POS systems protected by battery backup. A 6-VLAN network redesign is staged for the next maintenance window.
