About 406 Technical Services

Welcome to 406 Technical Services — your trusted technology advisor and support partner. Founded by Steve Akers, a seasoned telecom and network professional with decades of experience at companies like Sprint, Nextel, Clearwire, and Lumen, 406TS exists to simplify technology for businesses and households across Montana and beyond.

Our mission is twofold:

  1. To serve businesses as a strategic advisor, helping navigate complex IT decisions through vendor-neutral recommendations.
  2. To support the local community with approachable tech help, from Wi-Fi and smart TVs to PCs and printers.
Whether you're an enterprise evaluating UCaaS providers or a local customer needing better Wi-Fi at home, we bring the same commitment: honest advice, responsive service, and smart solutions.

Why Work With Us?

  • Plain-English Guidance: We translate tech into terms anyone can understand.
  • National-Scale Solutions: We partner with OTG Consulting to offer over 220 vetted providers.
  • No Vendor Lock-In: Every recommendation is customized to your actual needs.
  • One Point of Contact: You deal with us — we handle the rest.

Services Include:

  • Business IT Advising (Connectivity, Cloud, AI, Voice, Cybersecurity)
  • Home Tech Support (Wi-Fi, TVs, Computers, Printers)
  • Appointment Scheduling and On-Demand Support

Backed by OTG Consulting

As an OTG Consulting Partner, we have access to top-tier engineering resources, subject matter experts, and providers in all major technology swim lanes — from cybersecurity and cloud to IoT, AI, and automation.
